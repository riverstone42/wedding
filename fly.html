01 · Flight costs

Airfare Ranking By Airport (Low to High)

Ranked across the eleven states most of our guests are flying from — Kansas, Ohio, Michigan, North Carolina, Alabama, Indiana, Colorado, Virginia, Hawaii, Illinois, and Utah. These reflect typical routings, not live fares, so always compare your own dates.

1
Los Angeles LAX · Los Angeles, CALowest fares

Cheapest from nearly all eleven states. The big hubs — Chicago (Illinois), Denver (Colorado), Salt Lake City (Utah), Detroit (Michigan), Charlotte (North Carolina), and Honolulu (Hawaii) — all have nonstops to LAX, while the smaller markets (Kansas, Ohio, Alabama, Indiana, Virginia) connect efficiently through those same hubs. Most carriers, most competition, lowest fares.

2
Ontario ONT · Ontario, CA

The cheapest secondary airport, anchored by Southwest. Best value from its Southwest-heavy origins — Denver, Chicago-Midway, Salt Lake City, and Kansas — plus a new nonstop from Honolulu starting summer 2026. From the eastern and southern states it’s usually a pricier connection.

3
John Wayne SNA · Santa Ana, CA

Convenient to the venue but a fare premium, and usually a connection from most of these states. Nonstops do exist from a few hubs — Denver, Salt Lake City, and Chicago on Southwest, Alaska, or United — but you’ll pay for the convenience.

4
Long Beach LGB · Long Beach, CA

A compact, Southwest-focused airport with a small route map. Limited nonstops from this part of the country mean generally awkward routings and higher fares from the Midwest, South, and East.

5
Hollywood Burbank BUR · Burbank, CA

The most West-Coast-oriented of the group; it gains a Honolulu nonstop in 2026, but from the Midwest, South, and East it’s typically a connection and the priciest of the five.

04 · Rental cars

In the Airport, or a Shuttle Ride Away

At three of the airports you simply walk from baggage claim to your rental car. At the other two, the rental counters sit at a separate center you reach by shuttle — worth knowing if you’re tight on time.

John Wayne SNA · Santa Ana, CA
In airport · walk
Counters between Terminals A & B; cars in the adjacent structures.
Long Beach LGB · Long Beach, CA
In airport · walk
Counters in the Historic Terminal, steps from the concourse exit.
Hollywood Burbank BUR · Burbank, CA
In airport · walk
At the Regional Intermodal Transportation Center — a ~7-minute covered moving walkway from the terminal.
Ontario ONT · Ontario, CA
Shuttle to center
